Welcome intrepid mercenary

It's brave of you to have ventured into Gloomhaven, things are not all they appear. Perhaps you'll need some guidance along the way? I can see that the fates are not done with you. Great triumphs, great failures, supreme virtue, or cunning vice: I care not. Every adventurer requires a scribe, no? After an unfortunate incident (involving an errow to the knee) I'm not any help on the battlefield, but keep me in your employ and I'll make it worth your while.

I'll keep track of your accomplishments, to start - though my services don't stop there. My keen mind, suburb memory, and unparalleled navigation skills will undoubidly prove useful as well. I require only your indulgence until such a time as you're ready to give up your mantle as a mercenary and retire. Then I'll carry forward any knowledge we've gathered to aid the next adventurer that catches my fancy. I have a keen eye for the sort who are fated. Bound up with greatness.
